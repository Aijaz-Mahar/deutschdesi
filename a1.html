Lesson 1: German Greetings (Begrüßungen)

In German, greetings change based on the time of day and the level of formality. Below are the most common ways to say hello and goodbye.

German English Urdu (اردو) Hindi (हिंदी)
HalloHelloہیلو / سلامनमस्ते
Guten MorgenGood Morningصبح بخیرशुभ प्रभात
Guten TagGood Day / Afternoonآپ کا دن اچھا گزرےशुभ दिन
Guten AbendGood Eveningشام بخیرशुभ संध्या
Gute NachtGood Nightشب بخیرशुभ रात्रि
TschüssBye (Informal)اللہ حافظ / خدا حافظअलविदा
Auf WiedersehenGoodbye (Formal)پھر ملیں گےफिर मिलेंगे

Note: "Guten Tag" is the most common greeting used from midday until about 6:00 PM.
