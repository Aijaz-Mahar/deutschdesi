Lesson 2: The German Alphabet (Das Alphabet)

The German alphabet has the same 26 letters as English, plus 4 special characters. However, the pronunciation is different.

Special Characters (The Umlauts)

German Letter Name/Sound Urdu Equivalent Hindi Equivalent
Ä / äAeاے (A-ey)ए
Ö / öOeاوئے (Similar to 'O' with rounded lips)ओ (rounded)
Ü / üUeایو (Similar to 'U' in 'Music')यू
ßEszett (Double S)سین (Double S sound)स (Double S)
Desi Tip: The letter "W" in German is pronounced like the English "V" (e.g., Volkswagen is pronounced Folks-vagen).
